#+title: History is a narrative of communal impressions

- History is narrative composed of communal impressions of past
  events.[fn:1]
  - Impressions are lasting memories based on perception.
  - Such impressions may or may not originate with physical occurrences.
    Likewise, they may or may not reflect such physical reality
    accurately.

[fn:1] Nicholas Kotar (in-person conversation)
