#+TITLE: Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs

by Harold Abelson and Gerald Jay Sussman, with Julie Sussman

* [[info:sicp#3-2][sicp#3-2]]

Lexical scope is derived from the idea of environment model of
evaluation, where symbols are replaced with values from the most
immediate environment frame with a binding for the symbol.

** [[info:sicp#3-2-1][sicp#3-2-1]]

~define~ is a special form that binds symbols to environments (scopes).
