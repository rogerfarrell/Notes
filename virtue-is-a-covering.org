#+title: Virtue is a covering

According to St Basil the Great, the virtuous man does not want a
garment. For such a man, virtue serves as a garment.[fn:1]
[[file:nakedness-is-the-body-unveiled.org][He has no need to veil his body
to cover shame.]]

[fn:1] /Address to Young Men on the Right Use of Greek Literature/ by St
       Basil the Great,
